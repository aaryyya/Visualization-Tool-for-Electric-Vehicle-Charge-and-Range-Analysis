### Scenario 1: Charging Pattern and Usage Analysis

Aarav, a city energy planner, is responsible for managing electricity demand across urban areas. With the rise of electric vehicles, he needs to understand when and where people are charging their EVs. By using the Tableau-based visualization tool, Aarav can analyze charging data such as time of day, duration, and power consumed. Dashboards allow him to see peak charging hours, identify neighborhoods with high demand, and visualize usage trends on heatmaps. This insight helps him ensure grid stability, plan new charging infrastructure, and make data-driven decisions for sustainable city growth.

### Scenario 2: Battery Performance vs. Driving Range

Meera, a fleet manager for an EV taxi service, oversees a large number of vehicles where battery performance directly impacts daily operations. She needs insights into how battery health and charging cycles affect the actual driving range of her fleet. With the visualization tool, Meera can track metrics like state of charge, depth of discharge, and efficiency under different driving conditions. Tableau dashboards enable her to compare predicted versus actual range, spot underperforming vehicles, and anticipate when a battery may require replacement. This proactive, data-driven approach reduces downtime, lowers costs, and keeps the fleet running efficiently.

### Scenario 3: Comparative EV Model Efficiency Dashboard

Ravi, an environmentally conscious consumer, is looking to purchase his first electric vehicle. He wants to compare different EV models not just on price but also on performance, range, charging cost, and environmental impact. With the Tableau visualization tool, Ravi can interact with dashboards that compare efficiency across models, view cost per kilometer, and estimate long-term savings in fuel and emissions. The ability to filter by driving style and region provides him with personalized insights. This empowers him to make an informed, sustainable choice that matches both his budget and eco-friendly lifestyle.
